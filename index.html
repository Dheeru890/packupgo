FAQ

What is Pack Up +Go?
We're a travel brand that plans personalized vacations around the United States. The catch? Your destination is a surprise until the day you depart! Whether you choose to Staycation or to travel by plane or car, we will curate great recommendations for your Surprise Trip and book your accommodations. Want to know what our travelers are saying about Pack Up + Go? Head to our reviews page for insights from past travelers!
Where are you sending me?
Great question! But if we told you, that would ruin all the fun. We send our travelers to more than 300 Destination Cities across the US. Potential destinations for your Pack Up + Go Vacation do vary depending on the Trip Type you select. On average, transit times are 2-6 hours. If you have specific time or travel restrictions for your vacation, please let us know on the Pre-Trip Survey! All of our destinations have more than enough going on to keep you busy for the length of your vacation.
How do i know what to pack for my Surprise Trip?
We’ve got you covered! One week before you depart, we'll email you the weather forecast and a list of suggested packing items, such as a bathing suit or hiking boots, for your mystery vacation. The day before you depart, we’ll email you an updated weather forecast just in case anything has changed. All of our Plane Trips include one personal item and one larger carry-on bag for all travelers. All travelers get one smaller bag (purse, laptop bag, backpack) that will fit under the seat in front of you, as well as one carry-on suitcase that will fit in the overhead compartment.
What's the difference between budgets?
Your Surprise Trip Budget determines where we send you, how you get there, and where you stay. The primary factors to consider when choosing your budget are your travel dates and departure airport. If you're thinking of traveling around a major holiday, remember that flights and hotels are more expensive. Similarly, if you choose only regional or mid-sized airports, your budget options will be higher than if you were able to depart from a major airport. If there is money left in your budget after we have booked all the guaranteed aspects of your trip, we use those funds to book “add-ons,” such as restaurant or rideshare gift cards, tickets to attractions, and more.
Can travelers in the same group depart from different cities?
For Plane Trips, yes! This is one of the major benefits of a Pack Up + Go Trip. If each member of your group is departing from different places, it can get tricky to coordinate one destination - so we'll do it for you! On your Pre-Trip Survey, you’ll tell us which airport each traveler is departing from, and we’ll do our best to have your flights arrive at your destination around the same time. For our car-based Trips (Road Trips, Outdoors Road Trips, and Staycations) all travelers must depart from one location.
